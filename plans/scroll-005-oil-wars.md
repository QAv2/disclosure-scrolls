**Theme:** Standard Oil's rise, fake breakup, and reconsolidation. The illusion of antitrust.

### Content Blocks

In 1870, John D. Rockefeller founded Standard Oil. By 1904, it controlled 91% of US oil refining and 85% of final sales. When the Supreme Court ordered its dissolution in 1911, the public was told the monopoly was broken. It wasn't. It was distributed.

**Evidence Card 1** — DOCUMENTED

- **Source:** Corporate merger records (SEC filings); Rockefeller family trust documentation; Daniel Yergin, "The Prize" (1991).

There is no judicial review.
- **Source:** 35 U.S.C. sections 181-188 (full text); Federation of American Scientists (Steven Aftergood), annual Invention Secrecy Statistics; De Rassenfosse et al., "Secrecy Orders Impact" (ScienceDirect, 2023).
